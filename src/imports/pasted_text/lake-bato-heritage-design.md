Create a modern, immersive, culturally rich website design for a heritage project titled **“The Lake of Bato: Heritage of Rinconada.”**

The website should feel documentary-style, emotional, and interactive — combining cultural preservation, environmental awareness, and storytelling. The visual identity should reflect the atmosphere of Lake Bato in the Bicol Region: calm waters, fishing culture, local traditions, nature, and heritage.

STYLE & MOOD:

* Clean modern layout with subtle indigenous/Bicol-inspired accents
* Earthy and natural color palette:

  * Deep lake blue
  * Soft aqua
  * Warm beige/sand
  * Forest green
  * White backgrounds for readability
* Elegant typography with a cinematic storytelling feel
* Smooth scrolling and section transitions
* Semi-dark overlay on hero sections for readability
* Interactive documentary-inspired aesthetic similar to a digital museum or heritage archive

INTERACTIVE ELEMENT:

* Add a custom floating cursor inspired by the “Sinarapan” fish.
* The cursor should resemble a tiny translucent fish silhouette swimming smoothly across the screen.
* As users move their mouse, the cursor leaves subtle animated water ripple effects behind it.
* Ripples should be soft, minimal, and elegant — not distracting.
* The fish cursor should slightly “float” or sway naturally like it is moving through water.
* On hover over buttons or cards, the fish cursor gently reacts with a glow or ripple expansion effect.
